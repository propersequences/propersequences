# Proper Low-End Tools

Welcome to the **Proper Low-End Tools** sample pack repository! This pack contains a selection of high-quality kicks, subs, and basslines designed to help you achieve a powerful, clean, and well-defined low-end in your music productions. Along with the samples, you'll also find a mini book that covers advanced techniques for processing and shaping the low-end in your tracks.

Whether you're working on techno, house, or any genre that requires a solid foundation, these tools will help you craft the perfect bass frequencies.

## Contents:
- **Samples**: Kicks, subs, and basslines to lay down the perfect low-end foundation.
- **Mini Book**: Advanced techniques for EQ, compression, saturation, layering, and more.

Get ready to enhance your low-end production with Proper Low-End Tools!
